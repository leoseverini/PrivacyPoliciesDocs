# Privacy Policy for Kana Quest

**Effective date:** June 19, 2026
**Last updated:** June 19, 2026

Kana Quest ("the app", "we", "us") is a Japanese-language learning app developed
by **Leonardo Severini** ("the developer"). This Privacy Policy explains what
information the app handles and how your privacy is protected.

**Short version:** Kana Quest does **not** collect, store, or share any personal
information. There are no user accounts, no analytics, and no advertising. All of
your settings and progress are stored only on your own device.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We do **not** collect any personal information about you. Specifically, the app
does not:

- Ask you to create an account or sign in.
- Collect your name, email address, phone number, or location.
- Use analytics, tracking, or advertising services.
- Share any data with the developer or third parties for marketing.

## 2. Data Stored on Your Device

To make the app work and remember your preferences, Kana Quest saves a small
amount of data **locally on your device only**. This data never leaves your
device and is not transmitted to us or anyone else. It includes:

- Your appearance settings (theme, font, language).
- Your audio settings (sound effects on/off, selected voice).
- Your best quiz scores per practice group.
- Whether you have unlocked the optional full ("Premium") version.

This data is removed when you uninstall the app or clear its storage.

## 3. Permissions

- **Internet access** — used only to download the app's display fonts
  (via Google Fonts) the first time they are needed, and to support the device's
  text-to-speech engine. It is not used to collect or transmit personal data.

## 4. Audio / Text-to-Speech

Kana Quest uses your device's built-in **text-to-speech (TTS)** engine to
pronounce Japanese characters and words. The text sent to the TTS engine is
limited to the Japanese study content shown on screen and contains no personal
information. The behavior of the TTS engine (for example, Google
Text-to-Speech) is governed by the privacy policy of its provider and your
device manufacturer.

## 5. Fonts (Google Fonts)

The app uses the Google Fonts service to load the Japanese display fonts. When a
font is downloaded, Google may receive standard technical request information
(such as your IP address), as described in the
[Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). No personal
information from within the app is sent.

## 6. In-App Purchases

Kana Quest offers an optional one-time purchase to unlock the full version
(additional audio practice). Purchases are processed entirely by **Google Play
Billing**. The developer does **not** receive or store your payment details
(such as card numbers). Payment handling is subject to the
[Google Play Terms of Service](https://play.google.com/about/play-terms/) and the
[Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). The app only stores,
locally on your device, whether the unlock has been purchased.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Kana Quest is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal
information from anyone, including children. The app contains no ads, no chat,
and no social features.

## 8. Data Security

Because the app stores its data only on your device and does not transmit
personal information, there is no personal data held on any server controlled by
the developer.

## 9. Your Choices

- You can clear all locally stored data at any time by clearing the app's storage
  in your device settings, or by uninstalling the app.
- You can manage in-app purchases through your Google Play account.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on
this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after
changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
