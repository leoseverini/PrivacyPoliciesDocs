# Privacy Policy

## Information Collection and Use

This mobile application ("YahtzeeCounter") does not collect, store, or process any personal information directly from users. We do not require users to create accounts, provide personal details, or share any personal data to use this App.

## Third-Party Services

This App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain information for advertising purposes.

### Google AdMob

AdMob is an advertising service provided by Google LLC. AdMob may collect and process the following types of information:

- Device information (such as device model, operating system version)
- Advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID)
- IP address
- App usage information
- Location data (if location permissions are granted)

For more information about how Google processes your data, please review:
- [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Google's Advertising Privacy FAQ](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)

### Ad Personalization

You can opt out of personalized advertising by:
- **Android:** Go to Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID or Opt out of Ads Personalization
- **iOS:** Go to Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking

## Data Security

Since we do not collect personal information directly, there is no personal data stored on our servers. Any data collected by third-party services (AdMob) is governed by their respective privacy policies and security measures.

## Children's Privacy

This App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
