# Privacy Policy

**App:** Who Pays? (`com.leoseverini.whopays`)
**Developer:** Leo Severini
**Effective date:** 24 June 2026
**Last updated:** 24 June 2026

## Summary

Who Pays? is a **fully offline** app. It does not have user accounts, does not
connect to any server, and does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal
data. Everything you enter stays on your device.

## Information we collect

**We collect nothing.** The developer has no servers and receives no data from
the app. There is no analytics, advertising, tracking, or third‑party data
collection.

## Information stored on your device

The app stores the following **locally on your device only**, so it can work
without an internet connection:

- **Groups** you create (name, icon).
- **Members** you add (name, chosen emoji/color avatar).
- **Payment history** (amounts, dates, optional occasion labels) and the
  running balances used to keep the rotation fair.
- **Your profile** (a display name and an emoji/color avatar you choose).
- **App settings** (theme and language preference).

This data never leaves your device unless **you** choose to share it (see
below). Uninstalling the app removes all of it.

## Camera

The app requests camera access **only** to scan QR codes (to import a group or
add a person). Scanning happens entirely on your device. The app does **not**
take photos, does not store images, and does not send camera data anywhere.

## Sharing features (initiated by you)

- **QR codes:** the app can display a QR code that encodes a group or your
  profile so another phone can scan it. This is a local, screen‑to‑camera
  transfer; nothing is uploaded.
- **Share / Export:** you can export a group's history as a CSV file or share a
  QR image through your device's standard share sheet. The content goes only to
  the app or recipient **you** select. We are not involved in that transfer.

## Network access

The release build of the app declares **no internet permission**. It cannot
send data over the network.

## Third‑party services

The app uses the open‑source `mobile_scanner` library for on‑device QR/barcode
recognition. This processing happens locally on your device and does not
transmit data to the developer or any third party.

## Children's privacy

The app collects no personal information from anyone, including children.

## Your choices and data deletion

- Delete a group, member, or history entry at any time inside the app.
- Use **Settle up** to reset balances.
- **Uninstall** the app to permanently erase all locally stored data.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will be revised and the
updated policy will be published at the same location.
