# Privacy Policy for Papeleo PDF

**Last updated: July 14, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile application **Papeleo PDF** (the
"App"), developed by **Leonardo Severini** ("we", "us", or "our"), handles
information. By using the App you agree to the practices described here.

Papeleo PDF is an **offline-first** PDF toolkit. Your documents and images are
processed **locally on your device**. We do not operate any server that
receives, stores, or has access to your files.

---

## 1. Summary

- We **do not** require an account, name, email, or login to use the App.
- Your **PDF files and images never leave your device** for our processing —
  all merging, conversion, viewing, compression, and editing happen locally.
- We **do not** run our own analytics or collect personal data directly.
- The App shows **ads** (via Google AdMob) and offers a **one-time in-app
  purchase** (via Google Play). These third-party services collect certain data
  as described below.

---

## 2. Information we do NOT collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store:

- The **contents of your PDFs or images**.
- Your **name, email address, phone number**, or other contact details.
- **Account credentials** — the App has no user accounts.

The files you open or create are handled entirely on your device.

---

## 3. Information processed on your device

To provide its features, the App stores the following **locally on your device
only** (using on-device storage). This data is not transmitted to us:

- **File history** — a list of recently created/processed files so you can find
  them again.
- **App settings** — such as your theme choice (light/dark) and preferences.
- **Purchase state** — whether you have unlocked the Pro version.

This local data remains on your device and is removed when you clear the App's
data or uninstall the App.

---

## 4. Third-party services

The App uses the following third-party services, which may collect data
according to **their own privacy policies**:

### a. Google AdMob (advertising)

The free version of the App displays banner and interstitial ads through Google
AdMob. To serve ads, Google and its partners may collect and process data such
as your **device advertising identifier (Advertising ID)**, **IP address**,
**general device information**, and **app usage/interaction data**, and may use
this to show personalized or non-personalized ads.

- Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- How Google uses information from apps that use its services:
  https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites
- You can reset or limit ad tracking via your device settings
  (**Settings → Google → Ads**), including opting out of ad personalization.

Purchasing the **Pro version removes ads**, which stops AdMob from serving ads
within the App.

### b. Google Play Billing (in-app purchase)

The optional one-time **Pro** upgrade is processed by **Google Play Billing**.
Your payment is handled entirely by Google — **we never receive or store your
payment card details or billing information**. Google may share limited,
anonymized transaction information with us (such as whether a purchase was
completed) so we can unlock Pro features.

- Google Play Terms & Privacy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### c. Google Play Services

The App relies on Google Play Services (which supports ads, billing, and core
functionality). Data handling by Google Play Services is governed by Google's
Privacy Policy above.

---

## 5. Permissions

The App requests only the permissions it needs, and only when a feature uses
them:

- **Photos / files / storage** — to let you select images and PDF documents to
  process, and to save your results. Files are accessed only when you choose
  them and are processed on your device.
- **Camera** *(if used)* — to capture images for the Image-to-PDF feature. Photos
  are used only to create your PDF and are not sent to us.
- **Internet / network** — used only by third-party services (ads, in-app
  purchase). Your document processing does not require an internet connection.

You can revoke permissions at any time in your device's system settings.

---

## 6. Data sharing

We do **not** sell your personal data. We do not share your files or personal
information with third parties, except for the data collected directly by the
third-party services listed in Section 4 (Google AdMob, Google Play Billing,
Google Play Services) under their own policies.

---

## 7. Data security

Because your files are processed and stored locally on your device, their
security depends primarily on your device's own security (screen lock,
encryption, etc.). We recommend keeping your device and operating system up to
date.

---

## 8. Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age
required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information
from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through
a third-party service in the App, please contact us.

---

## 9. Your rights

Depending on your location (for example, under the **GDPR** in the EU/EEA or the
**CCPA** in California), you may have rights regarding personal data collected
about you. Because we do not collect personal data ourselves, most such data is
handled by Google. To exercise rights over data collected by Google's services,
please refer to Google's Privacy Policy and its data controls. For any data
stored locally by the App, you can delete it at any time by clearing the App's
data or uninstalling the App.

---

## 10. International users

The App can be used worldwide. Any data processed by third-party services
(Google) may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own,
in accordance with Google's privacy practices.

---

## 11. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by
updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of
the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
