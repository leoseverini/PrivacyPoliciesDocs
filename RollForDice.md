# Privacy Policy for Roll for Par

**Last updated: 19 July 2026**

Roll for Par ("the Game", "we", "us") is a single-player puzzle game developed
by Leonardo Severini. This policy explains what information the Game handles,
why, and what choices you have.

**Short version:** we do not collect, store or transmit any personal
information ourselves. Your progress stays on your device. The only data that
leaves your device is handled by Google, through the advertising and in-app
purchase services built into the Android version.

---

## 1. Information we collect

**None.** We operate no servers, no accounts, no analytics and no tracking of
our own. The Game does not ask for your name, email address, phone number,
contacts, photos, microphone, camera or location, and it has no code that sends
any information to us.

## 2. Information stored on your device

The Game saves the following locally, in its own private storage area, so that
you can pick up where you left off:

- Adventure progress, stars and unlocked levels
- Best scores and course records
- Achievement progress
- Your current in-progress round, so it can be resumed
- Power-ups held in your inventory
- Settings: volume, mute and language
- Whether you have purchased "Remove ads"

This data never leaves your device and is not readable by us or by other apps.
Uninstalling the Game deletes all of it. You can also clear progress and
statistics at any time from **Settings → Clear all stats** inside the Game.
(Clearing stats deliberately does *not* revoke a "Remove ads" purchase.)

## 3. Advertising (Android version only)

The free Android version shows banner and occasional full-screen ads served by
**Google AdMob**. To serve those ads, Google may collect and process device
information including an advertising identifier, approximate location derived
from IP address, and ad interaction data. This processing is carried out by
Google, not by us, and is governed by Google's own policies:

- Google Privacy Policy — https://policies.google.com/privacy
- How Google uses information from partner sites and apps —
  https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Ads served in the Game may be personalised, based on data Google associates
with your advertising identifier. You can turn personalisation off at any time
using the system-wide control described below; doing so does not remove ads,
but makes them non-personalised.

**Turning ads off:** the Game offers a one-time "Remove ads" purchase. Once
active, no ads are requested or displayed, and the ad SDK stops making
ad requests.

You can also limit ad personalisation system-wide in
**Android Settings → Google → Ads**.

The web and desktop versions of the Game contain no advertising and make no
network requests whatsoever.

## 4. Purchases (Android version only)

The "Remove ads" purchase is processed by **Google Play Billing**. Your payment
details are handled entirely by Google Play and are never seen by, sent to or
stored by us. We receive no payment information. The Game only asks Google Play
whether the purchase is owned by the signed-in Google account, and stores that
yes/no answer locally.

Google Play's handling of purchase data is described at
https://policies.google.com/privacy.

## 5. Children's privacy

The Game's content is rated for general audiences (ESRB Everyone, PEGI 3, USK
All ages) and contains no violence, no gambling, no chat and no user-generated
content. Its **target audience is users aged 13 and over**, as declared in the
Google Play Console. It is not part of the Designed for Families programme and
is not directed at children.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, and we collect
none at all — see section 1. If you believe a child has provided personal
information to Google through the advertising services described in section 3,
please use Google's controls linked there, or contact us and we will help where
we can.

## 6. Data sharing and selling

We do not sell, rent or share personal information, because we do not collect
any. The only third parties involved are Google AdMob and Google Play Billing,
described above.

## 7. Data retention and deletion

Because all data the Game creates is stored locally on your device, you control
its retention completely: uninstalling the Game removes it. There is no
server-side account or backup to delete, and no deletion request to send us.

For data processed by Google's advertising services, please use Google's own
controls linked in section 3.

## 8. Security

Game data is stored in the app-private storage sandbox provided by your
operating system, which prevents other apps from reading it. As we hold no data
off your device, there is no server for us to secure and nothing of yours for
us to lose in a breach.

## 9. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under the GDPR, CCPA or
similar laws to access, correct, delete or port your personal data, or to
object to its processing. As we hold no personal data about you, these requests
are best directed to Google for any advertising or purchase data it holds. You
may still contact us with any question, and we will help where we can.

## 10. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change and the
revised version will be posted at the same URL. Material changes will also be
noted in the Game's store listing release notes.
